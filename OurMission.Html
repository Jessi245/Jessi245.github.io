Our Mission:

We practice Natural Horsemanship Training on all our horses Training the horse to THINK instead of just REACTING And we LOVE doing it all BAREFOOT!

Whether its near the lake...

Whether its in the show ring or a parade!

Or IN the lake!

We susbscribe to the philosophy and practice of wet saddle blankets, and to train our horses to:

Flex and bend at the poll

Back on light rein

Neck rein

Forehand turn

Pivot

Side pass

Canter on correct leads

Cross any obstacle

Stay tied with paitence

Respect its handler and rider

Tolerate dogs, traffic, gun fire, and other surprises
